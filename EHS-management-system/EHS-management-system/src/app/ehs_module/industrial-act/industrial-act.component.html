INDUSTRIAL FACTORY ACT - 1948

Machinery or part thereof, or exempt, subject to such condition as may be prescribed, for securing the safety of the workers, any particular machinery or part thereof from the provisions of this section.



FACTORY ACT DESCRIPATION :

1)Unless they are in such position or of such construction as to be safe to every person employed in the factory as they would be if they were securely fenced

2)The headrace and tailrace of every water-wheel and water turbine

3) Any part of a stock-bar which projects beyond the head stock of a lathe

4) Unless they are in such position or of such construction as to be safe to every person employed in the factory as they would be if they were securely fenced
